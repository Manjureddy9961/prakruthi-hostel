Why Choose Prakruthi Hostels?

Established in 2017, Prakruthi Boys & Girls Hostels has been the benchmark for premium student accommodation in Puttur, Tirupati District. We understand that a peaceful mind is necessary for academic success.

We operate 6 independent Boys Hostels and 3 exclusively secure Girls Hostels, providing tailored environments. Our flagship offering is our Mess Facility with a 70+ seating capacity, where our Master Chef prepares hygienic, homestyle meals daily.
